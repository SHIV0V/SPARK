Help Center

Welcome to the Help Center! Here, you can find answers to common questions and learn how to use our platform effectively. If you need further assistance, feel free to contact our support team.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I search for universities and courses?

To search for universities and courses:

  • Go to the Home page.
  • Use the rotation bar to navigate to the Explorer.
  • Click on the search button to view search bar for searching detailed information about the university or course.

2. How can I explore career paths?

To explore career paths:

  • Navigate to the Search page.
  • Type the name of a profession or career in the search bar.
  • Click on the search results to view detailed information about the profession, including job responsibilities, required skills, and potential career growth.

3. What should I do if I encounter an issue?

If you encounter any issues while using the platform:

  • Visit our Support Page to submit a support request.
  • Provide your email address and a detailed description of the issue.
  • Our support team will get back to you as soon as possible to resolve the issue.

4. How do I contact support?

To contact support:

  • Go to the Support page.
  • Fill out the support form with your email address and a description of your issue.
  • Click Submit to send your request to our support team.
